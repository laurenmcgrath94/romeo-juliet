Romeo and Juliet

'What a way of bringing the community together!'
Romeo & Juliet is the FIRST outdoor and interactive musical to take place in Dublin 22. This new Romeo & Juliet is a hilarious modern day take on the 1595 tragedy play. What would life have been like if Romeo & Juliet had phones, social media or all of the other bad influences that take place in Dublin today?

This musical will take place in the Bawnogue field where you will be seated and asked to download an app. With this app you will be asked to determain the ending!
