Romeo and Juliet

About Us

Romeo anad Juliet is the first ever show to take place outdoors.

10EURO on Entry | 12 | JAN | 2020.

The lack of facilities in the area of Dublin 22 is frightening and is forcing the younger generation to get in with the wrong crowd and down the wrong path. Putting theirselves and others in danger. Romeo and Juliet want to bring the community together with a show based outdoors and raise money for charity.

We aim to connect with the teens in the area and make it as relatable, funny and modern. It will be based on a modern day Romeo & Juliet in the area caught up in a family feud over gangland issues.

How will the show plan out, well...

YOU DECIDE! With our downloadable APP on arrival you will determain the ending of this 'Gangland Romeo and Juliet' play. Will Romeo cross the Telebubby Hill to find Juliet? Come and find out!
